Field Safety

  • Inspect leaves, stems, and roots regularly.
  • Remove infected crop parts quickly.
  • Do not spray chemicals without guidance.
  • Wear gloves, mask, and boots when spraying.
  • Keep children away from treated areas.

Crop Care Tips

  • Use balanced irrigation and avoid water stagnation.
  • Maintain spacing between plants.
  • Track humidity and rainfall after storms.
  • Use light traps and sticky traps.
  • Keep a simple record of pest signs weekly.
